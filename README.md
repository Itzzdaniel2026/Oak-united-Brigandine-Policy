# Oak United ┇ Brigandine Policy

---

### **Requirements for Joining Oak United Trust**

For a school to become part of Oak United Trust, it must meet the following **Brigandine Policy standards**:

**[1]** A dedicated **staff room** must be available

**[2]** Fully equipped **English, Science, and general classrooms** must be in place

**[3]** Clean and accessible **toilet facilities** must be provided

**[4]** A suitable **dining hall** must be available for students

**[5]** A functioning **fire alarm system** must be installed and maintained

---

### **Staff Team & Governance**

The **Executive Headteacher of Oak United Trust** must hold an administrative role with permissions to:

* Access all communication channels
* Oversee and manage staff members across the trust

In cases of **reported misconduct**:

* The school’s internal staff team is expected to respond and take appropriate action
* If the school fails to address the issue adequately, **Oak United Trust will intervene**
* A designated trust representative will **lead the investigation and take necessary action**

---

**All member schools are expected to uphold these standards to ensure a safe, professional, and high-quality educational environment.**
